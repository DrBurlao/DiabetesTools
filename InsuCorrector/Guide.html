Welcome to InsuCorrector

InsuCorrector is a tool for calculating insulin dose based on current blood sugar, target blood sugar, and insulin sensitivity. Follow these steps to use InsuCorrector:

How to Use:

  1. Enter Current Blood Sugar: Enter your current blood sugar level (mg/dL) in the input field labeled "Current Blood Sugar."
  2. Enter Target Blood Sugar: Enter your target blood sugar level (mg/dL) in the input field labeled "Target Blood Sugar."
  3. Enter Insulin Sensitivity: Enter your insulin sensitivity (mg/dL per unit of insulin) in the input field labeled "Insulin Sensitivity."
  4. Calculate Insulin Dose: Click on the "Calculate Insulin Dose" button to calculate the recommended insulin dose based on the entered values.
