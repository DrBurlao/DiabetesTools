Welcome to Sugar Menu

Sugar Menu is an application designed to help individuals plan meals suitable for diabetes management. Follow these steps to use Sugar Menu:

How to Use:

  1. Adding Food Items: Enter the name and carbohydrate content of a food item in the respective fields and click "Add Food" to include it in the database.
  2. Creating Meals: Select the meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack), describe the meal, and specify the carbohydrate content. Click "Add" to add the meal to your plan.
  3. Viewing Meal Plan: See your planned meals categorized by type under "Meal Plan".
  4. Viewing Meal History: Check your meal history to track your consumption over time under "Meal History".
  5. Analyzing Data: Explore informative charts displaying carbohydrate consumption trends and meal types under "Informative Charts".
