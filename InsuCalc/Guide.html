Welcome to InsuCalc

InsuCalc is a tool designed to help manage insulin doses for diabetes treatment. Follow these steps to use InsuCalc:

How to Use:

  1. Calculate Basal Insulin: Enter your body weight in kilograms and click "Calculate basal dose" to determine your recommended basal insulin dose.
  2. Calculate Rapid Insulin: Select a meal and enter the grams of carbohydrates consumed, then click "Calculate rapid dose" to get your recommended rapid insulin dose.
  3. Configure Insulin/HC Ratios: Set insulin-to-carbohydrate ratios for each meal and save them by clicking "Save ratios".
  4. View History: InsuCalc maintains a history of calculated doses for your reference. Use the "Clear history" button to delete the history.
  5. Get Tips: Check out the "Tips for diabetes management" section for helpful advice on managing diabetes effectively.
